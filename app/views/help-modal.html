Answering and Saving

Click on the button to Save an answer. Note: Without saving, your answers will not be assessed.

Click on the button to Clear an answer. Note: Cleared answers are saved automatically.

  • Single Correct, True/False, Assertion Reasoning: Select the appropriate choice and click Save
  • Multiple Correct, Matrix Type: Select all the appropriate choices and click Save
  • Integer Type: Enter your answer in the field and click Save
Navigation
On Desktops:
  • Click on the / buttons at the bottom to go to the Previous/Next question
  • Click on the Question in the left drawer to go directly to that question.
On Smartphones and Tablets:
  • Click on the / buttons at the bottom to go to the Previous/Next question
  • You can also Swipe Left/Right to go to the Previos/Next question.
  • Click on the Drawer button on the top left corner to reveal the drawer And tap on the question you want to jump to.
Important
  • Do not reload or refresh this page under any circumstances
  • You Must save all your answers.
  • If you are done, Click on the button on the top right corner. Note, this will save All your answers, even the unsaved ones.
